Ethics Policy & Non-Disclosure Agreement

This document outlines the ethical standards and confidentiality obligations between Counsell Abroad and its approved referral partners.

1. Ethical Conduct

  • No false promises or misleading information to students
  • No fake documentation or unethical practices
  • Compliance with institutional and immigration regulations

2. Confidentiality

All student data, commission structures, institutional relationships, and internal processes are confidential and must not be shared without written permission from Counsell Abroad.

3. Non-Circumvention

Partners agree not to bypass Counsell Abroad to directly contact institutions or students introduced through this partnership.

4. Termination

Counsell Abroad reserves the right to terminate partnerships in case of ethical violations or reputational risk.

By continuing as a referral partner, you acknowledge and accept these terms.
