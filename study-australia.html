University Intake & Fees

Australian universities typically have intakes in February (Semester 1) and July (Semester 2). Tuition varies depending on the course and university:

  • Business & Management: AUD 28,000 – 38,000 per year
  • Engineering & IT: AUD 30,000 – 40,000 per year
  • Health Sciences: AUD 32,000 – 45,000 per year
  • Arts & Social Sciences: AUD 25,000 – 35,000 per year
