Why Study in Canada?

Canada is one of the top destinations for Pakistani students due to its high-quality education, diverse culture, and pathways to permanent residency. Universities focus on research, innovation, and employability.

Popular Courses

  • Business Administration & Finance
  • Engineering & Computer Science
  • Healthcare & Nursing
  • Environmental Science & Sustainability
  • Hospitality & Tourism Management
  • Arts, Media & Design
  • Data Science & Artificial Intelligence

Intake & Fees

  • Semester Intakes: January, May, September
  • Tuition Fees: CAD 18,000 – 40,000 per year depending on program
  • Scholarships: Merit-based awards CAD 1,000 – 15,000; some universities offer full tuition scholarships
  • Living Cost: CAD 12,000 – 20,000 per year

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form & personal statement
  • High school / college transcripts
  • English proficiency (IELTS 6.5–7.5, TOEFL 80–100)
  • Recommendation letters
  • Portfolio for creative programs (if applicable)
  • Proof of finances for visa purposes

Career & Post-Study Benefits

Studying in Canada provides:

  • Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP) 1–3 years for employment after graduation
  • Co-op and internship opportunities during studies
  • Pathways to permanent residency through Express Entry & Provincial Nominee Programs
  • Strong industry-university collaborations for career advancement
