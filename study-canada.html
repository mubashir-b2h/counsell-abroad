Why Study in Canada?

Canada is one of the most student-friendly countries, offering high-quality education, multicultural environments, and a clear path to permanent residency. Students benefit from affordable tuition compared to the USA, world-class universities, and opportunities for research and innovation.

  • Globally recognized universities with strong academic standards
  • Multicultural society and welcoming student environment
  • Work opportunities during and after studies
  • Safe and high-quality lifestyle

University Intake & Fees

Most Canadian universities offer two main intakes: Fall (September) and Winter (January). Tuition fees vary by program:

  • Engineering & Technology: CAD 20,000 – 35,000 per year
  • Business & Management: CAD 22,000 – 38,000 per year
  • Computer Science & AI: CAD 23,000 – 40,000 per year
  • Health Sciences & Life Sciences: CAD 25,000 – 45,000 per year

Admission Requirements

  • Official academic transcripts from previous studies
  • English proficiency tests (IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE)
  • Statement of Purpose and recommendation letters
  • Proof of sufficient funds for tuition and living expenses
  • Resume/CV for graduate programs
