Why Study in the USA?

The USA is a prime destination for Pakistani students seeking quality education. With world-renowned universities, flexible academic programs, and strong industry connections, studying in the USA opens doors to global career opportunities. Students gain exposure to cutting-edge research, internships, and networking.

Popular Courses

  • Business & Finance
  • Engineering & Technology
  • Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence
  • Health Sciences & Public Health
  • Law & Political Science
  • Media & Communication

Intake & Fees

  • Semester Intakes: Fall (August), Spring (January), Summer (May–June, limited)
  • Annual Tuition Fees: USD 20,000 – 45,000 (varies by university & course)
  • Scholarships: Merit-based and international student scholarships from USD 2,000 – 15,000

Admission Requirements

  • Completed online application with transcripts
  • Standardized tests: SAT / ACT for undergraduates, GRE / GMAT for postgraduates (some universities waive)
  • English proficiency (IELTS 6.5–7.5 or TOEFL 80–100)
  • Statement of Purpose / Essay
  • Letters of Recommendation (1–3)
  • Proof of financial ability
