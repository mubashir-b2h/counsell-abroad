University Intake & Fees

US universities typically offer multiple intakes: Fall (August/September), Spring (January/February), and some also offer Summer (May/June). Tuition fees vary widely based on the program:

  • Engineering & Technology: USD 28,000 – 45,000 per year
  • Business & Management: USD 30,000 – 50,000 per year
  • Computer Science & AI: USD 32,000 – 48,000 per year
  • Health Sciences & Medicine: USD 35,000 – 55,000 per year
