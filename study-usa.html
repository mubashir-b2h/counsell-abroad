Why Study in the USA?

The USA remains the most popular study destination for Pakistani students due to its unmatched academic excellence, cutting-edge research facilities, and global recognition. American universities emphasize critical thinking, innovation, and practical experience through internships and co-ops.

Popular Courses

  • Business Administration & Management
  • Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence
  • Engineering (Mechanical, Electrical, Civil)
  • Healthcare & Nursing
  • Biotechnology & Life Sciences
  • Arts, Media & Communication
  • Hospitality & Tourism Management

Intake & Fees

  • Semester Intakes: Fall (August/September), Spring (January), Summer (May)
  • Tuition Fees: USD 20,000 – 50,000 per year depending on university & program
  • Scholarships: Merit-based awards USD 2,000 – 25,000; some top universities offer full tuition scholarships
  • Living Cost: USD 12,000 – 18,000 per year

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application via Common App / university portal
  • High school/college transcripts
  • Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT for undergrad, GRE/GMAT for grad programs)
  • English proficiency (IELTS 6.5–7.5, TOEFL 80–100)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Statement of Purpose / Personal Essay
  • Portfolio for creative programs (if required)
  • Proof of finances for visa and living expenses

Career & Post-Study Benefits

Students in the USA benefit from:

  • Optional Practical Training (OPT) allowing up to 12–36 months work experience post-graduation
  • STEM graduates can extend work authorization for up to 3 years
  • Opportunities for internships with multinational corporations
  • Networking and research experience at globally recognized institutions
