Why Study in USA?

The United States is the world’s top study destination, offering cutting-edge research, flexible programs, and a global alumni network. Students gain exposure to top companies and innovative industries.

Popular Courses

  • Business & Management
  • Computer Science & AI
  • Engineering & Robotics
  • Medical & Healthcare Programs
  • Data Science & Analytics
  • Arts, Media & Design
  • Environmental Science & Sustainability

Intake & Fees

  • Semester Intakes: August (Fall), January (Spring), May (Summer)
  • Tuition Fees: USD 20,000 – 55,000 per year (depending on program & university)
  • Scholarships: Merit-based & need-based scholarships USD 1,000 – 25,000 per year; some universities offer full tuition awards
  • Living Cost: USD 12,000 – 22,000 per year

Admission Requirements

  • Completed online application
  • High school/college transcripts
  • Standardized tests (SAT/ACT for undergraduate; GRE/GMAT for graduate)
  • English proficiency (IELTS 6.5–7.5, TOEFL 80–100)
  • Recommendation letters & statement of purpose
  • Proof of funds for visa and tuition

Career & Post-Study Benefits

Studying in the USA gives:

  • Optional Practical Training (OPT) 1–3 years for work after graduation
  • Co-op programs and internships for real-world experience
  • Exposure to multinational companies and startups
  • Pathways for work visa sponsorship after graduation
  • Strong professional network globally
